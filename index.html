Key Takeaways & Next Steps

Q2 highlighted the power of video and local engagement on TikTok, while revealing areas for strategic improvement on other platforms. Our focus for Q3 is on fostering organic growth, optimizing existing efforts, and building a stronger presence on emerging channels.

Embrace Video-First Content

TikTok's success proves video is key. Integrate more short, engaging video content across Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn to capture attention and drive interaction.

Prioritize Organic Engagement

Reduce reliance on paid impressions by creating highly shareable and interactive content that naturally encourages likes, comments, and shares on Facebook and Instagram.

Strategic Channel Development

Invest in consistent posting and targeted campaigns for Instagram and X (Twitter) to build their follower bases and establish a stronger, more active presence.

Data-Driven Optimization

Continuously monitor performance metrics, especially on TikTok, to identify trends, understand audience behavior, and adapt strategies for maximum impact and sustained growth.
